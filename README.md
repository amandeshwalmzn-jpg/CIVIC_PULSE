# CIVIC_PULSE -  Community Issue Reporting Platform

CivicPulse is an AI-powered civic platform that empowers citizens to report 
local infrastructure issues (potholes, water leaks, broken streetlights, 
waste management) and track their resolution in real time.

Built on Google AI Studio and Gemini 1.5 Flash, it auto-categorizes issues 
from photos, detects duplicates, routes reports to the right government 
department, and predicts recurring problems before they escalate.

Citizens earn points and badges for reporting and verifying issues — turning 
civic responsibility into community collaboration.
